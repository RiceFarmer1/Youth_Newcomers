Welcome to Ontario!

This is a website designed to help youth immigrants navigate in their new country.








About Us



Although everyone who immigrates to a new country needs time to adjust, youths may struggle more than others.


As new student immigrants, it could be difficult for you to socialize with others, make friends, succeed in school, or get involved in your community. If any of these concerns are applicable to you, then you have found the right place.


Here at New Maple Youths, we have assembled a team of high school students who were once newcomers just like you. They endured through the tough circumstances and overcame challenges such as the language barrier, education, and fitting into the community. All of them are here to guide you through your journey as immigrant youths, sharing their own experiences and helping you adapt to the new environment in Ontario.
